## Big Number

- Pronounce numbers up to 1 followed by 309 zeros.
- The rest of the numbers follow a similar pattern, such as 101-illion (100 * 10^306), 102-illion (100 * 10^309), and so on.
- The base value (100) can be replaced with (10) or (1) and pronounced accordingly: 1, 10, 100 million.
