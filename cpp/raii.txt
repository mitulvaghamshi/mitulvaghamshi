RAII
- Resource Acquisition Is Initialization

RAIl Ensures:
- Automatic cleanup
- Exception safety
- No memory/resource leaks

Common RAIl classes in C++:

smart pointers:
- std::unique_ptr
- std::shared_ptr 

manage dynamic memory:
- std::vector
- std::string 

manage file handles:
- std::ifstream
- std::ofstream

manage locks:
- std::lock_guard
- std::scoped_lock
